Keymanager - ERC734

To fully benefit from the ERC275 standard you need to deploy an ERC734 smart contract. For starters this allows you to grant and/or revoke access to your account via one or many wallets.

Upgradable Security

The ERC-734 standard allows you to upgrade your security measurements in the future. Should the need for a more secure account arise, you can simply replace the implementation of your Keymanager.
