Level 0 - Status Quo

This is a very basic demonstration of ERC 725 in combination with ERC 734 .

Right now, all you have is your Wallet. Eg. a private and a public key.
By clicking on "Create Account" an ERC 725 Account will be created / deployed for you.

Level 1 - Proxy Account

Right now all you have is a plain and simple ERC275 proxy account.
The ERC734 key manager standard allows for more complex management of an ERC725 proxy account.
